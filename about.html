Our Story

Welcome to Momo Empire – your ultimate destination for authentic, steaming hot momos and delectable street food in Bhilwara! Founded with a passion for fresh, flavorful dumplings and innovative twists on classics, we bring the best of Himalayan and Indian street food to your plate.

From our signature KFC Momos and Pizza Momos to succulent Soya Chaap and Paneer Tikka platters, every dish is crafted with premium ingredients, made fresh daily. Our cozy spot at H242 MPS School to Kumbha Circle Road is where friends and families gather for unforgettable meals.
